Boygenius' latest album, "the record," released on March 31, 2023, showcases the collaborative talents of Phoebe Bridgers, Julien Baker, and Lucy Dacus. With emotionally intense tracks like "Cool About It" and "We're in Love," the album highlights each member's unique style and has been praised for its evolution from a side project to a full-fledged band.
